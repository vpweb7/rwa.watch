RWA.Watch is a daily monitor for tokenized real-world asset (RWA) protocols — tokenized treasuries, private credit, commodities, real estate and other on-chain RWA products. We track market value, 24H and 7D capital flows, category trends and rankings. Underlying TVL comes from DeFiLlama's open API; all flow calculations, coverage adjustments and rankings are computed by RWA.Watch.

See Analysis for daily and weekly recaps and About & Methodology for how the numbers are calculated. Facts only — no ratings.
