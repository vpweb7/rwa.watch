About & Methodology

RWA.watch aggregates publicly available data to monitor tokenized real-world asset (RWA) protocols. The platform does not provide investment advice or asset ratings.

Monitoring Signals

  • Low Coverage: monitoring signals may represent only part of a protocol’s TVL
  • High APY (reported): high-APY pools exist in the source dataset (often incentives/anomalies)
